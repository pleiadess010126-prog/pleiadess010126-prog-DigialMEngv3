## 7. GEO Optimization

### 7.1 Understanding GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

GEO optimizes your content for AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini).

**The 8 GEO Metrics:**

| Metric | Weight | What It Measures |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Directness | 15% | Clear, upfront answers |
| Authority | 15% | Citations, statistics, expertise |
| Structure | 15% | Headers, lists, scannable format |
| Conversational | 10% | Natural, engaging tone |
| Freshness | 15% | Timely references, recent data |
| Snippet Optimization | 10% | Featured snippet readiness |
| Semantic Richness | 10% | Entity coverage, topic depth |
| Readability | 10% | Ease of comprehension |

### 7.2 GEO Score Interpretation

```
A+ (90-100): Exceptional - Ready for AI citation
A  (80-89):  Excellent - Minor tweaks only
B  (70-79):  Good - Address recommendations
C  (60-69):  Fair - Significant improvements needed
D  (50-59):  Poor - Major revision required
F  (<50):    Fail - Rewrite completely
```
